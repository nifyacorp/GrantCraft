# GrantCraft: AI-Powered Research Grant Assistant

## Vision

GrantCraft empowers researchers to develop compelling grant proposals through an intuitive AI assistant that guides the entire process from initial concept to polished submission materials. By combining conversational AI with document generation capabilities, GrantCraft dramatically reduces the time and effort needed to prepare competitive research grants.

## Key Features

### 1. Intelligent Conversation Interface

- **Research Topic Exploration**: Discuss your research interests with the AI to refine your focus and identify funding-worthy angles
- **Contextual Guidance**: Receive suggestions tailored to your specific field, methodology, and target funding bodies
- **Continuous Iteration**: Refine ideas through natural conversation, with the AI adapting recommendations based on your feedback
- **Grant Matching**: Get suggestions for appropriate funding opportunities based on your research description

### 2. Document Generation & Management

- **Dynamic Document Creation**: Generate research plans, proposal drafts, and supporting materials directly from your conversations
- **Section-by-Section Approach**: Build your grant proposal incrementally, with AI assistance focused on specific sections (abstract, methodology, impact statement, etc.)
- **File Organization**: Automatically organize generated documents into a coherent project structure with version history
- **Template Library**: Access field-specific templates aligned with common funding requirements

### 3. Budget & Timeline Tools

- **Budget Generator**: Create detailed research budgets with appropriate categories and justifications
- **Interactive Timeline**: Develop realistic project timelines with milestones and deliverables
- **Resource Planning**: Calculate personnel needs, equipment costs, and other resource requirements
- **Visualization Options**: Generate Gantt charts and other visual representations of project planning

### 4. Collaboration Features

- **Team Permissions**: Share projects with collaborators at different permission levels
- **Comment System**: Leave feedback on specific sections of generated documents
- **Revision Tracking**: Monitor changes and improvements across proposal iterations
- **Export Options**: Share outputs in multiple formats (DOCX, PDF, XLSX, etc.) for easy distribution

### 5. Knowledge Enhancement

- **Funding Agency Insights**: Access information about grant reviewers' expectations and agency priorities
- **Best Practices Library**: Learn from successful proposals and incorporate proven approaches
- **Field-Specific Guidance**: Receive advice tailored to norms in your research discipline
- **Impact Framing**: Get help articulating the broader impacts and significance of your research

## User Experience

### Researcher Journey

1. **Initial Consultation**: Chat with the AI about your research interests and funding goals
2. **Project Structure**: Generate an outline and file structure for your grant proposal
3. **Section Development**: Work through each section with AI guidance, saving outputs as discrete files
4. **Supporting Materials**: Create budget spreadsheets, timelines, and visual components
5. **Refinement**: Iterate on generated content through continued conversation
6. **Final Assembly**: Compile materials into a cohesive grant package ready for submission

### Design Philosophy

- **Researcher-First**: Optimized for the unique needs of academic and industry researchers
- **Transparency**: Clear indication of AI-generated content vs. researcher input
- **Flexible Workflow**: Supports both linear and non-linear approaches to grant development
- **Time-Saving**: Reduces administrative burden so researchers can focus on scientific innovation
- **Learning-Oriented**: Helps newer researchers understand grant writing conventions while respecting the expertise of experienced grant writers

## Technical Capabilities

- **Natural Language Processing**: Understands complex research terminology across disciplines
- **Context Retention**: Maintains conversation history to provide relevant responses throughout the project lifecycle
- **Adaptive Document Generation**: Creates content aligned with both researcher input and funding requirements
- **Multi-Format Support**: Works with various document types required for comprehensive proposals
- **Secure Storage**: Protects sensitive research ideas and personal information

## Impact on Research Process

- **Democratizes Grant Access**: Makes grant writing expertise accessible to researchers at all career stages
- **Reduces Administrative Burden**: Automates routine aspects of proposal preparation
- **Improves Proposal Quality**: Helps researchers articulate ideas with clarity and impact
- **Accelerates Funding Cycles**: Shortens the time from research concept to submitted proposal
- **Facilitates Interdisciplinary Work**: Assists researchers exploring new fields or collaborative projects

---

GrantCraft envisions a future where researchers can focus on groundbreaking ideas while AI handles the administrative complexities of grant preparation. By streamlining the proposal development process, we aim to increase the time scientists spend on actual research rather than paperwork, ultimately accelerating scientific progress across all disciplines.
